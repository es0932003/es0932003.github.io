Meet the Hive Minded Team

Arabella

Hello, everyone! Welcome to our humble beekeeping page. My name is Arabella McEntire and I am twenty-one years old. Since I was a little kid, I have been enamored by insects of all kinds - even the creepy ones. I love bees and the impact they have on our ecosystem is enormous and understated. I am studying computer science and love designing web pages. I love gardening and make it a point to plant flowers that will attract local bees.

Aveline

Hi, my name is Aveline McEntire. I am a nineteen-year-old amateur apiarist. My friends and I started this company after meeting at OTC because we discovered we have a shared passion for the environment, for honey, and for bees. I come from a long line of beekeepers and can ensure that our product is top quality, as well as our ethics. Our practices are 100% ABF-compliant.

Emmaleigh

Hi! I am Emmaleigh Stone, and I am twenty-two years old. Although I have always loved both bees and honey -- the perfect complement to rolls! -- I have just recently gotten into bee keeping. I am currently studying at Ozark Technical Community College in hopes of attaining an associate's degree in computer information science. I spend most of my free time at 'Save The Bees' rallies and tending to my own precious little colony, The Boss Bees.

The Bee Conservancy is a non-profit organization that has donated hundreds of bee hives, opened many bee sanctuaries, and started the Ground Up campaign, and provided funding for scholarships that go towards prospective beekeepers. The ground up campaign is focused on protecting ground-dwelling bees, which make up 70% of the world's 20,000 bee species.
